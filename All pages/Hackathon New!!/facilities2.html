Facilities

CITY IN A CAMPUS

All the facilities of the city - in one campus!

SAFE AND SECURE RESIDENTIAL FACILITY FOR BOYS AND GIRLS

The university is committed to zero tolerance to ragging, alcohol, smoking, drugs and disciplinary misconduct. Automated turnstile gates with biometric machines regulate entry and exit to residential facility.

CLEAN AND GREEN ENVIRONMENT

Our Hostel is one of the greenest campuses in the country. The greenery makes for a great learning environment and promotes creativity!

IN HOUSE MENTOR SUPPORT

The residential faculty and staff provide after-hours guidance and support to the students, in case they need any academic or life advice.

Medical ASSISTANCE

The campus has a 24 hours 30 bed hospital which is staffed with resident medical officers, medical lab, dieticians and physiotherapists.

APARTMENTS STYLE HOUSING

Keeping students's requirements in mind, University offers quality residence (offering above average necessities) for their living comforts.

IN-HOUSE LAUNDRY

In house laundry services are available at a nominal charges to students.

EARN WHILE YOU LEARN

Become independent! Students residing in the campus can earn while studying by taking up part time jobs in labs, library, sales, shopping mall etc.
